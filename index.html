About Me

I’m Omar Youssef, a passionate Junior Data Scientist from Alexandria, Egypt, dedicated to transforming data into meaningful insights and intelligent solutions. I’ve completed the WorldQuant University Applied Data Science Lab, where I developed hands-on projects in data cleaning, feature engineering, machine learning, and model deployment using Python, SQL, and modern AI frameworks.

My interests include financial data analysis, risk modeling, time series forecasting, and business intelligence. I enjoy building predictive models, visualizing insights, and solving complex problems with data-driven approaches. Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s in Financial Engineering at WorldQuant University, aiming to bridge the gap between data science and quantitative finance.
